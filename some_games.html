Language Games Available

  • do-i-write-myself?

    A function that writes the code of all functions who do not write their own code. Does it write its own code?

  • thefarawaysummerevenings, Genetically Wherever

    Uses a genetic algorithm to direct a population of text towards phrases in John Ashbery's Whatever It Is, Wherever You Are

  • embed+this=wherever

    Short texts successively modified using GloVe word embeddings and the METAFONT algebraic typography system. In the draft sketch, the words in a sentence are diagrammed or constellated by their distance in the model, as words and character shapes change down lines of computed closeness

  • Room With Montague

    A playable model of a toy Montague-grammar semantic model.
